Writing

Writing is a tool that most of us know how to use. Humans have been writing for thousands of years to communicate ideas and information. Writing is very important, and this website will show you some writing technology you may not know about.

The Past

There are many writing hardware and software tools that people may not know much about. Here are some examples and explanations.

Hardware

BYOK

The BYOK is a small writing tool that helps with freewriting. It is also easy to carry while traveling.

Poke5

This hardware helps users write notes and safely save them for later.

Remarkable

This device can be used as a planner to help organize busy schedules.

Software

Scrivener

Scrivener helps organize writing and research in one place.

Ulysses

Ulysses helps give writing more style and supports blogs,images, and more.

Evernote

Evernote helps keep writing organized and allows users to leave comments and feedback.

Tool Idea

Quick Pen

A pen designed to help people with disabilities write. It uses a microphone to hear speech and write the words onto paper.
